About Us

Welcome to BuiBui!

Our team is dedicated to providing a reliable and efficient platform for managing bugs and issues in software development projects.


Our system is designed to support different roles, including testers, developers, project managers, and admins.

Each user has specific permissions and access levels that allow them to perform their tasks efficiently and collaborate seamlessly with other team members.

We understand the importance of effective communication and transparency in software development.

Our system allows users to track bug status, prioritize tasks, assign issues to team members, and request approvals from project managers.


Our bug tracking system is flexible and adaptable to different project management methodologies, such as Agile and Scrum.


Whether you're working on a small project or a large-scale enterprise application, our system can help you manage bugs and issues effectively.


Thank you for considering our system for your software development needs. We're committed to providing excellent customer support and continuous improvements to our platform.
